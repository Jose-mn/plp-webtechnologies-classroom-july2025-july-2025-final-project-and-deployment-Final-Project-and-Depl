Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it typically take to implement your solutions?

Implementation time varies based on the scope and complexity of the project. Smaller installations like solar panels for a small business can take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive sustainability transformations for large organizations may take 3-6 months.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with financial institutions to offer various financing options, including leasing, power purchase agreements, and green loans. We can help you find the solution that best fits your budget and financial goals.

What kind of maintenance do your systems require?

Our systems are designed for minimal maintenance. We offer ongoing maintenance packages that include regular inspections, performance monitoring, and necessary repairs. Most systems only require semi-annual or annual check-ups.

Do you provide services outside of Kenya?

Currently, we primarily serve clients in Kenya and East Africa. However, we do offer consulting services internationally and are expanding our operations. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and location.
